ARTEMIS Module

The ARTEMIS module was designed to test the operational characteristics defined in the MUSE module through simple simulations that provided subsides for calculating Key Performance Indicators (KPI).

The KPI preset, in a sense, provides a comprehensive picture of how these configurations performed in the scenario presented, allowing for a clearer understanding of their strengths, weaknesses, and overall suitability for the intended operational and strategic goals.

Once the simulation is only a simplified representation of reality, we encourage testing the configuration many times using ARTEMIS, in different demand scenarios, as well as applying slightly different inputs to MUSE to evaluate how operational configurations may change in different scenarios.

If you wish to re-analyze the performance of the selected operational values, click on "Re-start ARTEMIS module". If you want to re-evaluate the operational characteristics selected, select "BACK to MUSE module". Otherwise, if you are satisfied with the KPI values obtained, you may proceed to the following module, DELOS.
